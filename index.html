About Me

Hi! I'm Yagmur Delal Cengiz, a high school junior at Diyarbakir Bahcesehir Science and Technology College. I have a passion for physics and mathematics, dedicated to advancing my knowledge in these subjects. What fuels my drive is the desire to contribute to the betterment of my community, particularly in aspects it is suppressed or underdeveloped. Through various projects, I have endeavored to address pressing global issues, yet I am committed to augmenting the scale of my impact in the following years. Embracing the role of an entrepreneur, I find joy in maximizing project efficiency, crafting future plans, and developing business strategies, even for the simplest of my endeavors. I believe that entrepreneurship is the sole way of creating sustainable change. In my leisure time, I find solace in drawing and listening to music. Beyond my academic and entrepreneurial pursuits, I am a challenging chess player and an avid player on our local basketball team. Additionally, I enjoy drawing comics with friends. Do not hesitate to contact me regarding anything—from more information on my projects to engaging in discussions, even if it involves debating whether cats are anarchists.
